    About

    Programmers calculator lets you calculate in Base mode i.e, you can calculate in Decimal, binary, octal, or hexadecimal. It has also bitwise operators and you can also work in bits. You can also change its color by moving the slider to the top right corner. I just made this for fun while trying to implement javascript, please provide me with feedback and if you find a bug please report it, I will try to improve it.
